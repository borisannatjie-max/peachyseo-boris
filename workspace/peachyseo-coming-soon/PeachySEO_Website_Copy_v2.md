## Services Section

**Section Headline:**
SEO That Actually Works. No Games, No Gimmicks.

### Service 1 - Local SEO
**What it is:** We get your business found when people search "plumber near me" or "best restaurant in [your town]" on Google Maps and local search.

**Includes:** Google Business Profile optimization, local citations, review management, and location-specific landing pages.

**Perfect for:** Plumbers, Electricians, HVAC, Mobile Mechanics, Restaurants, Cafes, Salons, Dentists, Vets, Gyms

### Service 2 - Website SEO
**What it is:** We optimize your website so Google understands what you do — and shows you to customers searching for exactly that.

**Includes:** Keyword research, on-page optimization, technical fixes, content creation, and monthly reporting.

**Perfect for:** Any business with a website that wants more organic traffic.

### Service 3 - PPC (Google Ads)
**What it is:** We set up and manage Google Ads campaigns that actually convert — so you get real customers, not just clicks.

**Includes:** Campaign setup, keyword targeting, ad copy, bid management, and weekly optimization.

**Perfect for:** Businesses that want fast results while SEO builds over time.

### Service 4 - LLMO (AI Search Optimisation)
**What it is:** We optimise your business for how customers find you through AI search tools like ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews, and other large language models.

**Includes:** Structured data markup, entity optimization, FAQ content creation, N-E-E-A-T analysis, and AI-friendly content strategy.

**Perfect for:** Any business that wants to be found in the new era of AI-powered search.
