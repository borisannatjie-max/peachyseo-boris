## 🏆 DETAILED ANALYSIS

### 1. MOBILE MECHANICS ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (HIDDEN GEM!)

**Why #1?**
- Only 5-6 dedicated mobile mechanic businesses found in the entire Bournemouth/Poole area
- NOBODY is doing SEO for this niche
- Car breakdowns happen 24/7 = high urgency searches
- People search "mobile mechanic near me" when stranded = ready to buy
- No big chains dominating this space

**Search Demand (UK-wide):**
- "Mobile mechanic near me" = 18,100 searches/month
- "Car repair at home" = growing trend
- "Mobile car service" = steady demand

**Competition in Area:**
- ~5-6 businesses competing
- None appear to have optimized websites
- Google Maps presence is weak for most

**My Verdict: START HERE. Easiest wins.**

---

### 2. PLUMBERS ⭐⭐⭐⭐

**Why #2?**
- 50+ plumbers listed in Bournemouth area
- VERY high search demand
- But most haven't invested in proper SEO
- Emergency searches = instant customers
- Local "near me" searches are massive

**Search Demand (UK-wide):**
- "Plumber near me" = 135,000+ searches/month
- "Emergency plumber" = 22,200 searches/month
- "Blocked drain" = 14,800 searches/month
- "Bournemouth plumber" = solid local volume

**Competition in Area:**
- 50+ on Checkatrade alone
- But most rely on directory listings, not organic SEO
- Few have optimized Google Business Profiles
- Blog content is almost nonexistent

**My Verdict: Strong #2. Fast results possible.**

### 5. GYMS/FITNESS ⭐⭐⭐

**Why #5?**
- 22+ gyms in Bournemouth/Poole area
- Big chains (PureGym, The Gym Group) dominate
- Independent studios are easier targets
- Membership churn = constant need for new members

**Search Demand:**
- "Gym bournemouth" = high
- "Fitness studio near me" = growing
- "Personal trainer bournemouth" = niche but targeted

**Competition:**

**My Verdict: Good for niche targeting. Harder to rank for generic terms.**
